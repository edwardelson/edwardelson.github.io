About Me

Hello! I'm a PhD student at the Cambridge Centre for Data-Driven Discovery and Institute for Manufacturing, University of Cambridge. My current research interests are machine learning, network science, privacy preservation and operations research. I'm also the Chief Information Officer of Alva Energi. I used to work as a data scientist in Keysight Technologies. I obtained my bachelor degree in electrical engineering from the National University of Singapore.
