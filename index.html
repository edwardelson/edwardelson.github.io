About Me

Hello! I'm a quantitative researcher with background in network science, machine learning and electrical engineering. I'm currently pursuing a PhD at the Cambridge Centre for Data-Driven Discovery and Department of Engineering, University of Cambridge. My life philosophy is to explore, experience and build knowledge across different fields and expertises. This is reflected both in my general interest (intersection of research, education, finance and engineering), as well as my research works.
